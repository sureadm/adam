What is a letter

     A letter is a written message conveyed from one person to another through a medium. Letter can be written to seek for job or it can be written to send greetings to your loved ones. It can be hand-written or printed

Types Of Letter

letters are mainly classified into two. Namely;
>

Informal Letter

This type of letter is written to your beloved ones to inform them about your well-being or to convey your regards. Examples are; letter written to your father, mother, sister, e.t.c.

Formal Letter

A formal letter can also be called an official, this type of letter is conveyed between two individuals that are not related. Examples are; letter of application, letter of invitation, e.t.c.
